About Us

Codewithjameel is a community of developers who are passionate about the JavaScript language and its many applications. Our mission is to provide a platform for coders of all skill levels to come together, learn, share, and grow in their knowledge of JavaScript. The website was founded in 2021 by Jameel, a seasoned software developer with over a decade of experience in the industry. He recognized the need for a space where developers could come together to discuss and share their experiences with JavaScript. From there, Codewithjameel was born.
